I am a PhD Research Scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, BIT Mesra, Ranchi. Previously, I served as a Research Scientist II at AIIMS Raipur (Sept 2024 – July 2026), where I led a research team at the Department of Trauma & Emergency on the Intelligent Emergency Healthcare System project — developing critical care tools for remote hospitals, with a focus on real-time ECG analysis for early cardiovascular disease detection using deep learning and MLOps.

Previously, I was a Doctoral Researcher at IISER Bhopal's Department of Data Science and Engineering, contributing to urban informatics — building usage classification in Indian cities and predicting women's crime hotspots. My work bridges Computer Vision, Healthcare AI, and Geospatial AI.
